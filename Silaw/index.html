Empowering You to Save Energy and Fight Climate Change.

Electricity powers our homes, our lives, and our world. But with great power comes great responsibility. The majority of our electricity still comes from fossil fuels, which emit large amounts of carbon dioxide, contributing to climate change. Every light we leave on, every device we forget to unplug, and every overused appliance adds to this problem. At Silaw, we believe that small actions can lead to big changes.

Did you know?

Making the switch to energy efficiency isn't just about cutting costs though thats a great benefit, its also about reducing our environmental impact. Small, everyday changes can add up to a huge collective difference.

  • Nearly 40% of global carbon emissions come from energy production.
  • By 2050, if no changes are made, these emissions could double, leading to more extreme weather, higher global temperatures, and rising sea levels.
